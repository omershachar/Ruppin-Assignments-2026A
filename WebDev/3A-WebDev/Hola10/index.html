About

More About Us

We are two computer science students who have always had a natural passion for technology. One of us served in the army as an IT specialist, where he was exposed to the world of computers and coding on a deeper level, and today works in IT in a civilian company. The other works in QA, with a strong interest in automation, testing, and improving software quality. Together, we share a love for technology, continuous learning, and building tools that help solve real-world problems.

⭐ These Days! ⭐

These days, we are in our second year of our Computer Science degree, continuing to expand our knowledge and technical skills. We have experience with languages such as C and C#, and have also been introduced to Python. At the moment, we are focusing on Java, alongside core web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. As we progress, we're constantly exploring new concepts, strengthening our problem-solving abilities, and building a solid foundation for both software development and future specialization in the tech world.
