More About Us

We are two computer science students with a natural passion for technology. One of us served in the army as an IT specialist, where he was exposed to the world of computers and coding on a deeper level, and today works in IT in a civilian company. The other works in QA, with a strong interest in automation, testing, and software quality. Together we share a love for technology, continuous learning, and building tools that help solve real-world problems.

Our Favorite Picks in Tech & Life

From late-night podcasts to the books that keep us sane between assignments — here are our top picks
